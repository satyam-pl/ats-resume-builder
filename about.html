About CVCraft India

CVCraft India is a free, no-login ATS resume builder built specifically for Indian job seekers — freshers, experienced professionals, and everyone in between.

Why we built this

Most resume builders out there are either expensive, require you to create an account, add watermarks to your PDF, or are designed for Western job markets. We wanted to build something simple, honest, and genuinely useful for Indian candidates.

The Indian job market has its own norms — companies like TCS, Infosys, Wipro, and thousands of startups all use ATS systems. A fancy Canva template might look beautiful but will get rejected by these systems before a human ever reads it.

What we offer

  • Free ATS-friendly resume builder — no login, no account needed
  • Instant PDF download — no watermark
  • Resume guides written specifically for the Indian job market
  • Tips for freshers, IT professionals, and experienced candidates

Your privacy

We take your privacy seriously. Everything you type in the resume builder stays in your browser. We do not send your data to any server. We do not store your name, email, phone number, or any other personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.
