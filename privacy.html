Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how CVCraft India ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our website and free resume builder tool.

Information we do not collect

The CVCraft India resume builder runs entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the personal information you enter into the resume builder, including:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Work history
  • Educational background
  • Any other resume content

All data you enter stays on your device and is never sent to our servers.

Cookies and analytics

We may use standard web analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site. This data is anonymous and aggregated — it does not identify you personally. Analytics data may include pages visited, time spent on the site, and general geographic region.

Google AdSense

We display advertisements on this site through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Third-party links

Our site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through our website.
